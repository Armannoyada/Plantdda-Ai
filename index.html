Introducing PlantDDA

TRY PlantDDA

We’ve developed an AI-based plant disease detection system that helps users identify diseases in plants by analyzing images. Users can interact with the system by uploading or capturing at least three images of a plant, which the AI processes to verify if it’s a valid plant image before running the disease detection model.

The AI model, trained with TensorFlow and Keras, can detect various plant diseases across multiple types of plants. The system averages the predictions from all images to ensure greater accuracy in diagnosis. Users are also prompted to select the plant type to improve prediction precision.

We’re excited to introduce this tool and are looking forward to users’ feedback on its strengths and areas for improvement. Try it now at PlantDDA.COM.
